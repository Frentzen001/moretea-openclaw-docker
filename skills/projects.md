Garage@EEE Student Projects

Current innovator projects at Garage@EEE cover a wide range of ideas.

HeatSig incorporates thermal sensors into existing lighting to create smart lighting that tracks footfall and human traffic data for buildings.

Play Makers is an escape room experience combining a cryptex puzzle and a laser mirror maze where players reflect a laser onto a sensor to win.

Learnr is a platform focused on lifelong, accessible learning across a wide range of topics, creating a community for learners to discover and grow.

FRAM3D creates personalised 3D hologram souvenirs by scanning people or objects in a 360-degree photobooth and producing a portable holographic memento.

Team ReBina is a smart recycling bin that uses computer vision to sort waste correctly and reduce contamination in recyclables.

ESCAPEE is a remote-controlled escape room game where players navigate puzzles and challenges using a live video feed from an RC car.

Team Rexus is building a Mars Rover to compete in the University Rover Challenge, learning CAD, 3D printing, ROS, PCB design, and AI along the way.

Garage 2.0 (BEHeRe) is a blockchain-based electronic health record system designed to make healthcare data in Singapore more secure and patient-controlled.

REYSS is a fall detection system using an IR camera to detect when elderly residents fall and automatically alert their caregivers via a notification.

Wunderkids is building an automated commode potty washer to reduce the manual cleaning workload for nurses and lower exposure to bacteria.

Quick Checkout is a mobile app that lets shoppers scan items and pay without queuing, eliminating the need for POS machines or self-checkout kiosks.

DIP E026 Escape Room Design integrates technology into escape rooms with feedback-enabled hardware that is easy to reset and operate with minimal manpower.
