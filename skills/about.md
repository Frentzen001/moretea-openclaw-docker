Garage@EEE

Garage@EEE is a student-led makerspace in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at NTU. It gives students the environment, materials, and funding they need to develop ideas and build things alongside their technical skills. With strong industry connections and a large alumni network, Garage creates not just engineers but thinkers and entrepreneurs.

The objective is to encourage innovation and cultivate entrepreneurship among EEE students, and to instill a passion for engineering and ideation in the community.

There are two main tracks. The Ambassador Track gives students a platform to propose and run initiatives for the NTU community across six portfolios: Branding and Marketing, Business Development, Operations, Start-Up, Training and Development, and Welfare.

The Innovator Track exposes students to the startup and maker community, helping them bring their own ideas to life. Students come in with startup ideas and get guidance, funding, and facilities to make them real through programmes like Tinkering Projects, Innotrack, and Launchpad.
