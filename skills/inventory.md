Garage@EEE Inventory — Where to Find Things

Use this file when a visitor asks where to find a specific item, tool, component, or piece of equipment.
Refer visitors to the zone name, not shelf codes.

---

## Heavy Machines Room

Tools and equipment for making and electronics work.

**Safety gear:** Safety goggles, rubber gloves, fabric gloves, latex gloves, ear plugs.

**Hand tools:** Screwdrivers, pliers, wire cutters, wire strippers, hammers, large set squares, magnifying glass.

**Fabrication:** Acrylic bender, heat gun, drilling equipment and tool heads, painting equipment.

**Soldering and rework (backup):** Backup soldering stations, rework stations, soldering consumables. Primary units are in the Electronics Workbench.

**Electronics workbench (backup):** Backup multimeters, oscilloscopes, waveform generators, DC power supplies. Primary units are in the Electronics Workbench.

**Components and consumables:** Jumper wires, jumper cables, LED strips, LED (green superbright), USB-A connectors, assorted electronic components, natural gas sensor, sandpaper, glue guns, fabric, PCBs, batteries, electromagnet.

**Power:** Extension cords, reeled extension cords, power strips.

**Other:** Drone equipment with accessories, dust pans.

---

## Electronics Workbench

The main area for electronics work and soldering.

**Soldering and rework:** Soldering stations, soldering stands, rework stations, lead soldering wire, wire wrapping wire, solder tips.

**Test equipment:** Multimeters, oscilloscopes, waveform generators, DC power supplies, breadboards, probes and testers, transmitters.

## 3D Printing Room

Everything related to 3D printing, resin printing, and associated equipment.

**Filament and 3D printing:** Filament reels (multiple types and colours), filament holders, filament recyclers.

**Resin printing:** Resin printer equipment, Formlabs 4L flex build platform, resin printing consumables.

**Materials and storage:** Acrylic sheets, wood, 3D printed items, arts and craft equipment.

**Electronics and computing:** Raspberry Pi and accessories, desktop computers, keyboards, mouse, label printer, 7-segment display, UPS external battery.

**Other:** Ultrasonic cleaner, safety goggles, cleaning equipment, assorted power cables, DVR unit.

## Component Towers — Electronics Components

Two component towers in the electronics area, each with four sides of drawers.

- **Resistors** — wide range of values (ohms to megaohms), including potentiometers
- **Capacitors** — ceramic, electrolytic, and film capacitors across a wide range of values
- **Logic ICs** — 74LS series (TTL), 74xx series, and 4000-series CMOS
- **Op-amps and comparators** — general-purpose and precision op-amps
- **Voltage regulators** — fixed and variable regulators
- **Transistors** — NPN and PNP bipolar transistors, MOSFETs
- **Diodes** — rectifier diodes, zener diodes, bridge rectifiers, SCRs, thyristors
- **Microcontrollers and ADC/DAC ICs** — AVR, PIC, and various converter ICs
- **Sensors** — IR sensors, touch sensors, tilt sensors, obstacle sensors, gyro/IMU, barometric pressure
- **Connectors and headers** — male/female header pins, crimp terminals, DC jacks, IDC connectors, housing shells
- **Switches** — lever, micro, toggle, button, magnetic, DIP switches
- **Passives and misc** — LEDs, relays, buzzers, fuses, optocouplers, rotary encoders, ferrite beads, ceramic resonators
- **Hardware** — M3 to M12 screws, nuts, washers, standoffs
