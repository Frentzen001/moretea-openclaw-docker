Garage@EEE Programmes

There are three main programmes under the Innovator Track: Tinkering Projects, Innotrack, and Launchpad.

Tinkering Projects supports students who want to explore a technology or build a mini-project. It runs for one semester and participants attend bi-weekly update sessions. At the end of the semester, Tinkerers present their final products in a sharing session. Both individuals and groups can apply as long as at least one team member is an NTU EEE student. Funding of up to $200 is available. You can sign up by filling in the proposal form at: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfBO3ExuCFVuyCJnqHZfhHmptDWa1BFW6OCBFJHRu_4_tcGpw/viewform

Innotrack is for students with a startup idea who want more serious support. Teams get funding of up to $999 as well as access to facilities and mentorship. You do not need to have a fully formed idea to join — you can come in and work with other students and Garage will provide the same level of support.

Launchpad is the most advanced programme, also providing funding up to $999 plus access to all Garage facilities. Like Innotrack, you can join even if you do not have a startup idea yet and collaborate with other members.
