# Garage@EEE Guided Tour Script

## Tour intro

Say this when starting a tour:

"Welcome to the Garage@EEE tour. We will visit eight spots — the Entrance, Fabrication Lab, Machines Lab, Top 10 Office, Kirchoff's Pod, Maxwell's Pod, the Project Showcase Wall, and the Exit. Let's get started."

---

## Canonical stop order

1. entrance
2. fabrication_lab
3. machines_lab
4. top_10_office
5. kirchoffs_pod
6. maxwells_pod
7. showcase_wall
8. exit

---

## Transition phrases

Say one of these after the narration at each stop, before navigating to the next:

- After entrance → "Let me take you to the Fabrication Lab first."
- After fabrication_lab → "Next up is the Machines Lab just across the way."
- After machines_lab → "Let's head to the Top 10 Office."
- After top_10_office → "Now I'll show you Kirchoff's Pod."
- After kirchoffs_pod → "One more pod to go — Maxwell's."
- After maxwells_pod → "Almost done — the Project Showcase Wall is worth seeing."
- After showcase_wall → "Last stop is the exit."

---

## Tour outro

Say this after the final stop (exit):

"That is the full Garage@EEE tour. If anything caught your interest, I am happy to go back for a closer look, or just answer questions from here."
