Garage@EEE Ambassador Portfolios

There are six ambassador portfolios at Garage@EEE.

Welfare and Outreach handles community bonding. They organise events for Garage members, run CIP projects to inspire young engineers, and host delegations and guests on lab tours from around the world.

Branding and Marketing maintains Garage's image. They create posters, graphics and content to attract potential ambassadors and sponsors, and develop communication and design skills.

Operations keeps the makerspace running. They operate and maintain all equipment, manage inventory, and provide logistical support for all events and initiatives in Garage.

Business Development manages external relationships. They correspond with stakeholders, scout contacts on LinkedIn, write professional emails, and build Garage's network.

Startup Development nurtures innovation among students. They guide aspiring tinkerers and innovators in taking their first steps, with support from experienced mentors who help bring projects to life.

Training and Development builds technical skills in the community. They become trainers and facilitators for workshops, push themselves to learn new skills, and serve as makeathon mentors for participants.
