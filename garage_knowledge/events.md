Garage@EEE Events and Competitions

Garage@EEE organises several events throughout the year — some internal for members, some open to all NTU students, and some open to students across Singapore.

---

STARTathon

STARTathon is Garage's internal makeathon-style robotics competition. Students design, build, and compete with custom robots. The theme changes each year — past editions have featured battlebots and soccerbots, and this year's challenge is a line-following robot. It is one of the best entry points for freshmen and new members to get hands-on experience with robotics in a friendly, competitive setting. It develops hardware, programming, and teamwork skills.

Keywords: STARTathon robotics competition line-following battlebots soccerbots internal freshmen

---

GEEEnius

GEEEnius is Garage's internal orientation camp for members. It brings the whole team together early in the academic year to build relationships, understand each other's strengths, and set the foundation for collaborative projects and initiatives throughout the year. It is not a competition — it is a bonding and team-building experience.

Keywords: GEEEnius orientation camp internal bonding team building members

---

Enitio

Enitio is a Freshmen Orientation Programme (FOP) run by Garage@EEE under the Faculty of EEE. The flagship activity is a large-scale Treasure Hunt that mixes laser tag and scavenger hunt elements across the NTU campus, using a self-built game controller called the Wanderers. Enitio helps freshmen settle into university life, make new friends, and experience something hands-on and exciting right from the start. It is open to incoming EEE freshmen.

Keywords: Enitio freshmen orientation programme FOP treasure hunt laser tag Wanderers game controller campus freshmen EEE

Innovation Festival

The Innovation Festival is Garage's anniversary showcase and networking event. Industry professionals, faculty, staff, alumni, and community partners come together to see the best student projects from the year. Outstanding teams get to present their ideas to experienced professionals, making it a strong networking and visibility opportunity for student innovators. It celebrates what Garage has built over the year.

Keywords: Innovation Festival showcase networking alumni industry professionals anniversary projects presentations

---

Escendo

Escendo is Garage's flagship makeathon camp. Participants build a working prototype around a given theme, learn technical skills through workshops run during the camp, and pitch their work to judges at the end. It is an intensive multi-day event that brings together students, industry professionals, and innovators to collaborate and create under time pressure. It is one of the most challenging and rewarding events Garage runs.

Keywords: Escendo makeathon camp prototype workshops pitch judges intensive multi-day flagship

---

IdeasJam

IdeasJam is a nationwide ideathon organised by Garage@EEE. Participants develop innovative solutions for a specific theme, supported by workshops and mentorship to sharpen their thinking and pitching skills. Unlike most Garage events, IdeasJam is open to students from all universities across Singapore, not just NTU. It is a great opportunity to meet students from other institutions and compete on a wider stage.

Keywords: IdeasJam ideathon nationwide Singapore all universities open workshops mentorship pitching

---

Makerspace Friendlies

Makerspace Friendlies is a joint event between Garage@EEE and SUTD Makerspace. The 2026 edition is called Rapidfire Rover and runs over two days. Day one is held at NTU and involves an autonomous maze challenge, where your rover must independently navigate and map an unknown layout without human control. Day two is held at SUTD and switches to a head-to-head arena battle where you pilot your rover manually and use power cores collected during day one as ammunition to score points against other teams. It combines autonomous robotics with competitive remote-control action.

Keywords: Makerspace Friendlies SUTD Rapidfire Rover autonomous maze rover arena battle remote control two days joint event 2026
