The Innovator Track has three programmes: Tinkering Projects, Innotrack, and Launchpad. All three are designed to help students bring ideas to life with funding, facilities, and mentorship. You do not need a polished or complete idea to apply — Garage is here to help you develop it.

---

Tinkering Projects

Tinkering Projects is for students who want to explore a technology, test out an idea, or build a small-scale project. It is a great starting point for students who are new to making or who want to try something without the full commitment of a startup programme.

The programme runs for one semester. Participants attend bi-weekly update sessions to share progress and get feedback. At the end of the semester, Tinkerers present their final products in a showcase and sharing session.

Both individuals and small groups can apply. At least one team member must be an NTU EEE student. Funding of up to $200 is available for materials and components.

To apply, fill in the Tinkering Projects proposal form. The link is available in the Garage@EEE Toolbox (find it in the GARAGE101 topic on the Garage@EEE family Telegram group).

Innotrack

Innotrack is for students who have a startup idea and want more serious, structured support to develop it. Teams receive funding of up to $999 as well as access to all Garage facilities and mentorship from experienced advisors and alumni.

You do not need a fully formed idea to join Innotrack. You can come in at an early stage, work alongside other members, and Garage will provide the same level of support to help you develop your concept into something real.

Innotrack is well-suited for teams with a clear problem they want to solve, even if the solution is still being figured out.

Keywords: Innotrack startup idea $999 funding mentorship facilities serious support early stage teams

---

Launchpad

Launchpad is the most advanced programme in the Innovator Track. Like Innotrack, it provides funding of up to $999 and full access to all Garage facilities. Launchpad is intended for teams that are ready to take their project to a higher level of development or that are working toward a more ambitious goal.

As with Innotrack, you can join Launchpad even if you do not yet have a startup idea — you can collaborate with other Garage members and develop one here with Garage's support.
